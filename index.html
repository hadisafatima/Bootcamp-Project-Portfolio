About Me

Hello! My name is Hadisa Fatima Syed, and I am currently a Software Engineering student in my 2nd semester at Mehran University of Engineering and Technology, Jamshoro.

Since May 2023, I have been deeply involved in Front-End Development, and I have gained considerable confidence in working with CSS to create visually appealing websites. I can confidently style any HTML code, be it mine or someone else's, and create beautiful web layouts.

Recently, I've been actively learning JavaScript to enhance my development skills further. While I'm still familiarizing myself with libraries beyond Bootstrap, I can already design responsive websites that look great on various devices.

I am enthusiastic about continuing to learn and grow in the field of Front-End Development. Building aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites is not just a passion but a journey of constant improvement.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and feel free to explore my projects and achievements to see my progress and dedication in this exciting field.

My works/ Experience

I started learning Front-End Development in May 2023 and have since honed my skills through online platforms. During this journey, I completed a couple of projects that allowed me to apply my knowledge effectively.

Project 1: E-Commerce Platform

My first project was an E-Commerce Platform, inspired by the layout and functionalities of "FLIPKART." The main goal of this project was to strengthen my proficiency in CSS properties and rules. Through this project, I gained valuable experience in building responsive and visually appealing web pages.

Project 2: Perks of Reciting HOLY QURAN and Following SUNNAH

For my second project, I developed a website highlighting the benefits of reciting the Holy Quran and following the Sunnah. Users could navigate through all 114 surahs of the Holy Quran and read about the specific benefits of each surah. Additionally, the website provided insights into the Sunnah of our beloved Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم. This project allowed me to showcase my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills while creating a meaningful and educational web application.

You can find both of these projects on my GitHub profile. Feel free to explore them through the links provided in the contacts section or beneath my profile picture.
